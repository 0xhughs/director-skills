# Story, Voice, and Release QC

Use this for full story/film packages, not just isolated prompts.

## Story check

- Does every scene advance conflict, character, theme, or necessary setup?
- Does the ending pay off planted details rather than adding new explanation?
- Is the scale appropriate for the target runtime?
- Are beautiful AI-generated moments serving the story, or distracting from it?

## Voice check

- Does the prose/dialogue still sound specific, or has it become generic AI smoothness?
- Are there cliches, overwritten feelings, or interchangeable speakers?
- Do dialogue lines survive being read aloud?
- Are sensory details specific to character and setting?

## Continuity check

- Are golden images, bible entries, shot prompts, and final clips using the same asset IDs?
- Are prop, costume, time-of-day, and damage states consistent?
- Did model transfer alter identity, palette, or setting geography?

## Release hygiene

- Document prompts, reference sources, model/tool versions, manual edits, and final selection decisions.
- Confirm rights for stock, references, voices, likenesses, music, and generated outputs.
- Preserve provenance metadata such as C2PA/SynthID where available.
- Treat legal/copyright guidance as a risk checklist, not legal advice.
