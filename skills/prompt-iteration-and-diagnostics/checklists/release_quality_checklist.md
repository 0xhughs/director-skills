# Release Quality Checklist

- Story: every scene advances conflict, character, or theme.
- Voice: dialogue/prose avoids generic AI phrasing and on-the-nose emotion.
- Continuity: character, costume, prop, location, light, and palette states match the bible.
- Performance: dialogue is short enough to speak and sync naturally.
- Post: color, grain, audio bed, subtitles, and upscaling are planned.
- Rights: references, voices, likenesses, music, and stock assets are cleared for the intended use.
- Provenance: prompts, settings, outputs, manual edits, and metadata are archived.
