# Example: Genre to Visual Direction

## Input
A 4-minute grief mystery set in an apartment building basement. Tone: quiet dread, intimate, not flashy.

## Visual direction
- Camera: restrained, mostly locked-off or slow dolly-in; close-ups only at decisions; wide shots used for isolation in hallways.
- Lens: 35mm for basement geography, 50mm for human perspective, macro inserts for keys/cassettes/dust.
- Lighting: motivated green-white fluorescent overheads with small flashlight beams; no beauty lighting; shadows retain detail.
- Palette: oxidized green, old brass, navy uniform, blue dawn accent.
- Texture: chipped paint, dust, tarnished metal, damp fabric, natural skin texture, faint film grain.
- Blocking: the guard begins centered and controlled, then drifts toward frame edges as doubt grows.

## Prompt-ready style block
Quiet psychological mystery realism, restrained locked-off frames and slow push-ins, cool green fluorescent practical light, old brass and chipped paint textures, natural skin and fabric detail, faint film grain, no glossy CGI finish.
