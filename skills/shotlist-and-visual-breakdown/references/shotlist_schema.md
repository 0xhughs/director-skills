# Shot List Schema

Recommended columns:
- Shot ID
- Scene beat
- Dramatic purpose
- Subject/action
- Shot size/framing
- Camera angle
- Lens feel
- Camera movement
- Blocking
- Lighting/color
- Production design/props
- Audio/dialogue
- Duration estimate
- Continuity notes
- Asset/reference needs
- Prompt handoff notes

Keep one dominant subject/action and one dominant camera behavior per AI-video shot unless the model supports multi-shot control.
