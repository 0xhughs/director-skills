# Camera Plan

## Choose camera by purpose
- Geography: wide, stable, readable blocking.
- Emotion: close, shallow depth, restrained movement.
- Suspense: negative space, slow reveal, limited information.
- Action: tracking, low angle, clear spatial vectors.
- Detail: insert or macro, static or very slow push.

## Continuity
Track eye lines, screen direction, prop hand, wardrobe state, and location orientation. Use re-establishing or insert shots after major spatial changes.
