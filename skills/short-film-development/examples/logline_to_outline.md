# Example: Logline to Short-Film Outline

1. Opening image: Guard checks empty hallway monitors; one screen flickers with static.
2. Pressure: The song starts behind the storage door at 3:17 a.m.
3. First attempt: He radios the owner, who orders him to ignore it.
4. Complication: Dust moves under the door in rhythm with the song.
5. Discovery: His mother's cassette label appears in the logbook.
6. Choice: He unlocks the door despite losing his job.
7. Payoff: Inside is not a ghost, but a room of tenant memories scheduled for disposal at dawn.
8. Final image: He carries one cassette into blue morning light.
