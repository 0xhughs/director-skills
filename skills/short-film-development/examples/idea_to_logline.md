# Example: Idea to Logline

## Input
A lonely apartment building night guard hears a song from a locked storage room every night.

## Workflow output
Premise route: psychological mystery about grief and duty.

Logline: When a sleep-deprived night guard hears his late mother's favorite song from a locked storage room, he must decide whether to open a door the building owner forbids before sunrise, but each clue suggests the room is preserving a memory he may not survive losing again.

Theme question: Is closure an act of courage or a second abandonment?

Visual anchors: fluorescent corridors, brass key ring, dust under storage door, old cassette player, blue dawn light.
