# Continuity Handoff

When story development ends, hand off these anchors:
- Character IDs, stable appearance, wardrobe baseline, emotional arc.
- Location IDs, geography, time-of-day rules, lighting baseline.
- Prop IDs, story function, initial state, state changes.
- Visual tone, palette, realism level, camera language.
- Scene list with purpose, emotional turn, and prompt-worthy visual anchors.
