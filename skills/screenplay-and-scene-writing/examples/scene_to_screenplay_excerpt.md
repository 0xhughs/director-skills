# Example: Scene to Screenplay Excerpt

```text
INT. BASEMENT HALLWAY - NIGHT

The guard's flashlight trembles over a row of storage doors. The song leaks from 12B, thin and warped.

He fits a brass key into the lock. Stops.

GUARD
You picked a terrible night.

No answer. The cassette warbles, slower now, as if the batteries are dying.

He turns the key.
```

Handoff: storage door 12B, brass key, warped cassette audio, flashlight tremor, blue-green fluorescent light.
