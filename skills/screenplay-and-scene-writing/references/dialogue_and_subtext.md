# Dialogue and Subtext

## Dialogue does
- Pursues a tactic.
- Conceals or reveals pressure.
- Changes relationship distance.
- Buys time, attacks, seduces, deflects, tests, pleads, or traps.

## Dialogue avoids
- Explaining theme.
- Saying exactly what the character feels unless collapse or confession is the beat.
- Repeating information already visible.

## Revision pass
For each line, label the tactic. If two lines perform the same tactic, cut or combine one.
