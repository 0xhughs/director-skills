# Scene Construction

## Scene transaction
Every scene should answer:
- Who wants what right now?
- Who or what resists?
- What tactic is used first?
- What changes the tactic?
- What is gained, lost, revealed, or damaged?

## Beat workflow
1. Entry state.
2. First tactic.
3. Resistance/interruption.
4. Escalation or reveal.
5. Choice under pressure.
6. Exit image or changed state.

## Visual writing
Write what can be seen or heard. Replace "she feels guilty" with behavior: she wipes the clean glass again, avoids the photo, or pockets the receipt.
