# Audio, Motion, and Timing

## Audio layers
- Dialogue: exact quoted line, speaker, emotional delivery, language, lip-sync note.
- Foley/SFX: footsteps, cloth, glass, rain, machinery, door, breath.
- Ambience: room tone, traffic, crowd murmur, wind, insects, fluorescent hum.
- Music: mood and evolution, not copyrighted song titles.

## Motion realism
Write physical verbs and consequences. A fall has weight; a cup spill changes the table; a turn shifts fabric and hair; a door slam creates vibration and sound.

## Duration guidance
- 4 seconds: one gesture or simple reveal.
- 6 to 8 seconds: one action plus consequence.
- 10 to 15 seconds: one compact beat, or simple multi-beat if supported.
- Longer narratives: chain clips with final-frame/first-frame continuity.
