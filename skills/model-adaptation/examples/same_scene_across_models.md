# Example: Same Scene Across Models

Scene: a woman in a yellow raincoat looks through a rainy cafe window, holding a steaming cup.

Universal intent: quiet longing, warm interior/cold exterior contrast.

Image model prompt: medium close-up still, 50mm lens, warm cafe practical light against blue rainy window, natural skin texture, condensation and steam.

Video model prompt: 6s, she exhales and lowers her gaze as steam curls from the cup; camera performs a very slow dolly-in; rain streaks continue on glass; ambience of muffled rain and cafe room tone.
